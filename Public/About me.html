About Me

Background

Jazzam R. Ambagan currently serves as a Criminal and Civil Docket Clerk in the judiciary, where he brings professionalism,
attention to detail, and a strong affinity for technology to support the efficient flow of court operations.
Born in 1998 in Barangay San Jose, Sipalay City, he relocated with his family to Hinoba-an in 2009, his father’s hometown.
The eldest of three siblings, Jazzam has always embraced leadership and responsibility. One of his siblings is now a teacher,
while the youngest is pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering. He is happily married to his wife, April Rose Elloso, and
together they are blessed with a son, Xavier Jazz. Jazzam earned his Bachelor of Science in Criminology from Negros Oriental
State University – Bayawan-Sta. Catalina Campus, and successfully passed the Criminology Licensure Examination in June 2019.
Outside of his professional life, Jazzam has a passion for sports, particularly table tennis, as well as computers and modern
technology. These interests have helped him develop practical skills that he effectively applies in his daily work as a docket clerk.
